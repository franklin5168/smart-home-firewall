##### START #####


1. Power on
    - SmartThings Hub: Power on
    - D-Link camera: Power on
    - Philips Hue: Power on
    - TP-Link plug: Power on
    - Xiaomi camera: Power on

2. Control with phone on OpenWrt-5GHz network
    2.1. D-Link app
        - Start app
        - Stream camera for 10 seconds
    2.2. Hue Essentials app
        - Open app
        - Turn on
        - Change color
        - Change intensity
        - Turn off
    2.3. Kasa app
        - Open app
        - On
        - Off
    2.4. Xiaomi app
        - Open app
        - Stream camera for 10 seconds while moving
        - Stream camera with sound
        - Use microphone
        - Take screenshot
        - Record video
        - Move camera
        - Turn off
        - Turn on
    2.5. SmartThings app
        - Open app
        2.5.1. SmartThings plug
            - Turn on
            - Turn off
        2.5.2. Philips Hue
            - Turn on

            - Change intensity
            - Turn off
        2.5.3. TP-Link plug
            - On
            - Off
    2.6. Alexa app

            - Turn on
            - Turn off
        2.6.1. Philips Hue
            - Turn on
            - Change color
            - Change intensity

            - Off
